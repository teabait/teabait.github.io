Tea Ho is a writer

and queer Viet-American refugee whose notable accomplishments include surviving life in a frat house for over two years, getting inducted into a now-defunct secret society, and receiving a phone call from their uncle — after he died.

Tea currently writes for HBO's The Sympathizer. Before that, Tea staffed on Hulu's We Were the Lucky Ones.

Tea got their start as a fellow in Lena Waithe's inaugural Hillman Grad Mentorship Lab and a mentee in Giant Leap's Development Accelerator. In their past lives, Tea worked as a web developer, designer, and wedding photographer. Tea's writing balances dark truths with levity to tell socially-conscious stories. Come laugh and cry with them.
